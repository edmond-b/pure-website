3D Modeling

From the age of 14 I have been playing around with 3D software, starting with modeling basic environments with Sketch-Up to creating characters such as the one you see above in Blender. I've come a long way since the early days and have picked up important skils such as low poly counts and clean topology - meaning the character you see is ready for animation.
